SEVENTH.AI

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY PROJECT MANAGEMENT PLATFORM

OVERVIEW

  • Tools: Sketch, Material UI Sketch library, React, Jira
  • Deliverables: Market Landscaping, User Research, High Fidelity Screens, Usability Tests
At Seventh we are used AI and ML to help startup founders develop a high level IP strategy by curating personalized competitor insights, conducting prior art search, and providing an IP valuation estimate. This information helped them identify gaps and provide a better pitch to investors, show that they have done their due diligence, and have a better chance at receiving funding.

PROBLEM

Startups run into legal issues further down the road that can be extremely messy to resolve if they do not have their IP properly protected. Why?
As a startup founder, they have many other priorities that hold greater immediate importance than having their IP sorted out. The common culprits are:
  • not enough time/money
  • legal things are confusing
  • hiring a lawyer is expensive
How might we help founders do their due dilligence before it's too late?

SOLUTION

"Turbo Tax for IP". Simplify the patent filing process and provide valuable insights about their market space so that they can focus on their company mission and make it a success.

MY ROLE

Working closely with the Head of Design and a Seventh Co-Founder, I focused on the high level IP strategy design for our founder user persona. Before beginning the design process, I conducted many user interviews and sent out surveys to get a better understanding of what startup founders struggle with, what they would like to see in our product, and at what stage we would be able to provide them with the most value. As a team, we also set up experiments to validate or invalidate hypotheses.
With this information, I designed high fidelity screens and conducted usability tests to see what people were most interested in and what kind of information they expected to find after clicking on certain elements. Designs were done in Sketch using the Material UI library. I wrote in-depth acceptance criteria so that design handoff to developers would be smooth. I also helped code some screens in React when our developers had other stories on their plates. Our primary focus was usability and functionality in order to have a working product by demo day.
